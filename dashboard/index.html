Setup & Safety Notes

1

Common Ground

All modules — ESP32, GPS, relay, sensors — MUST share a common GND. Isolated grounds cause garbage serial data.

2

GPS Outdoors

The NEO-6M needs clear sky view. Indoors it stays searching (solid LED). Take it outside or near a window.

3

Relay Failsafe

Wire the ignition through relay COM → NC. This means if ESP32 loses power, the circuit stays closed (engine stays startable — failsafe).

4

Vehicle Power

Use an LM2596 buck converter to step down 12V car battery → 5V ESP32 Vin. Never connect 12V directly.

5

WiFi Credentials

Store WiFi/API keys in a separate config.h and add it to .gitignore. Never commit secrets to GitHub.
